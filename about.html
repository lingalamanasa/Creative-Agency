Our Story

A Decade of Creative Excellence

Stackly was born in 2015 from a simple belief: every business deserves a brand that truly represents its potential. What started as a small team of three passionate designers in Hyderabad has grown into a full-service creative powerhouse with over 60 specialists across design, technology, and strategy.

Over the past decade, we've partnered with startups, mid-size companies, and Fortune 500 enterprises across 25+ countries, delivering over 500 projects that have collectively generated billions in client revenue.

Our approach is simple: we listen deeply, think boldly, and execute flawlessly. Every project is a new opportunity to create something the world hasn't seen before.
